About

Architecture and Nature Intertwined

25-floor standalone building that features 150 exclusive residencies: view flats, townhouses with patios, and penthouses with terraces.

Bionic architecture, natural finishings, ultramodern technologies, your own wellness-center with pool and spa — all in one riverside building next to a park.

Springs is a transparent masterpiece, elegantly woven into the park and urban landscape.

Our sophisticated residential complex elevates its residents above the ordinary, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the capital from its observation terraces and delivering unparalleled premium-class comfort.

Nature becomes your life’s interior with a green belt of parks, surrounding landscaped gardens, plant décor of the loggias, and the ivy-draped private patios.
