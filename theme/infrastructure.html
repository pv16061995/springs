Our elegant Wellness-center is a crown jewel of Springs, possessing it keeps you youthful. Warm and inviting light of the lobby transports you to a carefree resort. Marbles steps of the pool, caressing steam of the hammam, light appetizers at the waterside cafe — here relaxation originates.

Our yoga space is meant for thoughtful relaxation, and our fitness center — for active recovery. Step onto the fitness terrace after an energizing workout and recline on a sun lounger among flower beds. It’s easy to show yourself love.

Park-like Parking

At Springs, everything has been marked by designer’s hand that turned utility spaces aesthetic objects. Our two-level designer car park is framed with a natural stone fence, its textures subtly accentuated by a chain of opalescent sconces. Encircled by trees and dramatic silhouettes of flowerbeds, this parking will make your car sparkle like a jewel meant for the opulent lifestyle.
