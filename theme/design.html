Harmony in Greenery
and Glass

Springs resembles streams of transparent air and clear water sculpted into an asymmetrical glass tower that soars towards the sky. Wave-like longlines wrap around the facade and reveal verdant terraces that offer a vantage point for observation and introspection.

Aristocratic Quartet

Glass, metal, stone, and wood — the four elements that define the essence of Springs, an airy yet durable structure.

High-clarity glass makes our building appear levitating, while stone and wood allow you to feel the essence of time — time that you'll wish to halt again and again to admire the elegance that adorns your life.
